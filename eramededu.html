Who We Are

EramedEdu is a sister company of EramedGroups, dedicated to importing world-class educational equipment for schools, universities, and vocational institutions across Ethiopia. Our goal is to modernize learning environments with quality, affordable tools.

With over a decade of experience in educational solutions, we bridge the gap between global educational technology providers and Ethiopian institutions, ensuring you get the best equipment at competitive prices.
